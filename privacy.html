Privacy

Shikaku MVP does not collect analytics or ad telemetry.

The app stores gameplay state in your browser for functionality:

  • In-progress puzzle state
  • Local best times (top 50 per puzzle)

When you sign in, the app sends your first name and best level completion times to the leaderboard API.

The leaderboard stores one best completion time per user per level.

To clear data, remove site storage for this domain in your browser settings.
